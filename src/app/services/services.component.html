IT Managed Services

Managed Services allows a business to offload IT operations to a service provider, known as a Managed Services Provider. The managed service provider assumes an ongoing responsibility for design, develop, monitor, manage and/or problem resolution for the IT systems. MindPROS provides advice, offers honest estimates, and treats sensitive issues with efficiency and professionalism. MindPROS has the experience helping organisations make the transition, and help them to see the benefits of increased efficiencies, improved productivity and minimised downtime.

We provide the following IT Managed Services:

  • Networking & Infrastructure
  • Security Management
  • Communications Services
  • Software-as-a-Service
  • Data Analysis

Staff Augmentation

IT staff augmentation services are ideal for stringent budget and time-bound projects. It is quite common that managing, grooming, training, retaining and taking care of people take up many company resources. The staff augmentation program at MindPROS gives the clients the access to best resources and skillsets in the industry without the need to hire or train them. By providing the best IT resources, MindPROS gives you full control over the project and complete flexibility to scale up or down based on the requirements. MindPROS will allow you to add or subtract resources without the liabilities that are required for full time permanent employees.

What kind of staff?

MindPROS has a highly dedicated, qualified, and trained team of professionals who thoroughly understand various technologies and the needs of the clients. Our strength lies in identifying candidates with ideal blend of technical skills and relevant experience. Our professional experience will become competitive advantage for the clients.

What is our process of hiring?

The process of hiring from MindPROS is simple where in the client gives out the requirememts and expectations from the professional. Based on the requirements, MindPROS recruiters find candidates, interview them, and provide the most relevant candidates for client to review. The client can interview candidates and select the professional resources. MindPROS will provid an all-round onbaording process and handle all administrative services.
